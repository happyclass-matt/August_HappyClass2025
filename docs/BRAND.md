Warm, plainspoken, empowering. Caregiver + Sage.

## Visual
Ivory canvas, soft blues, gold accents. Rounded shapes, soft shadows, calm motion.

## Microcopy Patterns
- Buttons: verbs, present tense (e.g., "Create class")
- Empty states: one‑line purpose + single CTA
- Errors: human + helpful + next step
- Success: brief; confetti sparingly and motion‑respecting
